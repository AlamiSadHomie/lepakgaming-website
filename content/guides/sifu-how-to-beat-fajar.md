## Introduction

Fajar the Botanist is the first major boss you'll encounter in Sifu, and he can be brutal if you don't know his patterns. This guide will help you defeat him efficiently while keeping your death counter low.

## Boss Overview

Fajar is encountered at the end of The Squats level. He's a former student who uses powerful kicks and unpredictable timing. Despite being the first boss, he punishes button-mashing hard.

**Key Stats:**
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Recommended Age:** Under 30 (to save deaths for later bosses)
- **Arena:** Small enclosed room with minimal space
- **Phases:** 2 phases with different movesets

## Phase 1: Understanding His Patterns

### Main Attacks to Watch For

**1. Spinning Heel Kick**
- Fajar's signature move
- Telegraphed by him turning his back
- **Counter:** Dodge forward and to the side, then punish with a combo

**2. Low Sweep Combo**
- Quick low attacks that trip you
- **Counter:** Jump or use high block, avoid dodging backward

**3. Running Charge**
- Fajar dashes across the arena
- **Counter:** Sidestep and sweep him as he passes

**4. Guard Break Grab**
- Unblockable red glow attack
- **Counter:** Dodge away immediately, do NOT try to block

## Winning Strategy

### The Patience Approach (Recommended for First Victory)

1. **Keep Your Distance**
   - Don't rush in blindly
   - Let Fajar commit to attacks first
   - React rather than attack randomly

2. **Focus on Parries**
   - Parrying fills his structure bar faster
   - Hold L1 and tap L1 just before impact
   - Practice timing on his basic punches first

3. **Punish After Dodges**
   - Dodge his spinning kick
   - Land 2-3 quick strikes
   - Back off before he counters

4. **Use Environmental Takedowns**
   - Grab Fajar (hold Square + X)
   - Throw him into walls for bonus damage
   - Builds structure meter quickly

### The Aggressive Approach (For Experienced Players)

1. **Constant Pressure**
   - Stay close to limit his kick range
   - Use fast jabs to interrupt his attacks
   - Mix in sweeps to break his guard

2. **Chain Takedowns**
   - Build focus meter through combos
   - Use focus attacks when structure is high
   - Sweep → Focus attack → Combo

## Phase 2: The Rage Mode

After breaking Fajar's structure the first time, he becomes more aggressive.

### Changes in Phase 2

- Attacks are faster
- New charging tackle move
- Less recovery time between combos
- More feints and fakes

### Phase 2 Strategy

1. **Play More Defensively**
   - His increased speed makes parries harder
   - Focus on dodging over blocking
   - Create distance when needed

2. **Bait His Charge**
   - Back up to trigger his running tackle
   - Sidestep and punish with sweep
   - Easiest opening in phase 2

3. **Use Weapons If Available**
   - Staff or bat from earlier in level
   - Weapons deal massive structure damage
   - Can end fight quickly

## Essential Tips

### Before the Fight

- **Practice in Training Mode**
  - Available from main menu
  - Learn parry timing without consequences
  - Familiarize yourself with Fajar's moves

- **Bring a Weapon**
  - Staff is best (from second floor)
  - Bat works too (ground floor)
  - Don't waste durability on regular enemies

- **Unlock Useful Skills**
  - Crotch Punch (cheap but effective)
  - Strong Sweep Focus
  - Charge Impact for guard breaks

### During the Fight

- **Manage Your Structure**
  - Your structure bar = your guard meter
  - When full, you get stunned
  - Reset it by avoiding damage for a few seconds

- **Don't Panic**
  - If you're getting combo'd, hold block
  - Wait for an opening, don't mash dodge
  - One good counter is worth 10 failed attacks

- **Save Your Focus**
  - Don't waste focus on regular hits
  - Use it when his structure is almost full
  - Focus attacks = guaranteed structure damage

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Button mashing** - Fajar will parry and counter you  
❌ **Staying in corners** - You need space to dodge  
❌ **Blocking everything** - Your structure will fill too fast  
❌ **Chasing him** - Let him come to you  
❌ **Using focus attacks randomly** - Save them for structure breaks  

## Recommended Skill Loadout

1. **Crotch Punch** - Fast interrupt
2. **Strong Sweep Focus** - Best focus attack for this fight
3. **Environmental Mastery** - More damage from throws
4. **Parry Impact** - Extra structure damage on perfect parries
5. **Charged Backfist** - Strong guard break option

## Final Phase Tips

When Fajar's health is critical:

1. **Don't Get Greedy**
   - He doesn't get stronger at low health
   - Take your time
   - One mistake can reset the fight

2. **Finish with Structure Break**
   - Build his structure to full
   - Perform a takedown for style points
   - More satisfying than chip damage finish

3. **Stay Calm**
   - This is when most players die
   - Maintain your strategy
   - Victory is close!

## Post-Fight Reward

Defeating Fajar unlocks:
- Progress to The Club level
- Fajar's outfit (if done at age 25 or younger)
- Shortcut for future runs

## Conclusion

Fajar is all about learning patterns and staying patient. Don't expect to beat him first try - each death teaches you his moves better. 

**Average clear time:** 2-5 minutes  
**Expected deaths (first playthrough):** 3-7  
**Recommended age to finish:** Under 30  

Good luck, and remember: structure is key. Break his guard, and Fajar falls easily.
